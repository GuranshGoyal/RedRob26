# Senior AI Engineer - Founding Team

## Things you absolutely need

- Production experience building embeddings-based retrieval systems: sentence-transformers, BGE, E5, OpenAI embeddings, embedding drift, index refresh, retrieval-quality regression.
- Production experience with vector databases or hybrid search infrastructure: Pinecone, Weaviate, Qdrant, Milvus, FAISS, OpenSearch, Elasticsearch.
- Strong Python and production code quality.
- Hands-on experience designing evaluation frameworks for ranking systems: NDCG, MRR, MAP, offline-to-online correlation, A/B test interpretation.
- 5-9 years of experience with senior engineer judgment; shipped at least one end-to-end ranking, search, or recommendation system to real users.
- Career history primarily at product companies, not pure services or consulting firms.
- Located in or willing to relocate to Pune, Noida, or another Tier-1 Indian city.

## Things we'd like you to have

- LLM fine-tuning experience: LoRA, QLoRA, PEFT.
- Learning-to-rank models: XGBoost-based or neural.
- Exposure to HR-tech, recruiting tech, or marketplace products.
- Background in distributed systems or large-scale inference optimization.
- Open-source contributions in the AI/ML space.

## Things we explicitly do NOT want

- Title-chasers with frequent company switches under 1.5 years.
- Framework enthusiasts whose work is mostly LangChain tutorials or demo projects.
- Candidates who have only worked at consulting firms in their entire career.
- Primary expertise in computer vision, speech, or robotics without significant NLP or information retrieval exposure.
- Work entirely on closed-source proprietary systems for 5+ years without external validation.
- Candidates from pure research environments without any production deployment.
- Recent under-12-month LangChain/OpenAI-only experience without substantial pre-LLM-era ML production.
- Senior engineers who have not written production code in the last 18 months.
